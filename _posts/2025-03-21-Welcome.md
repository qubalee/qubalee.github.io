# Overview

Hello! My name is **Abdullah Alqubalee**, and I'm a PhD geoscience researcher. My current research focuses on exploring various geological processes to understand how they shape the Earth's surface and subsurface, and how they impact the quality and availability of natural resources.

**Alqubalee Notes** serves as a comprehensive repository for my research in geoscience. Here, you will find a variety of resources, including my notes on integrated research topics, summaries of my published articles, code for data analysis and visualization, and the datasets utilized in my work. Additionally, I provide tutorials, documentation, announcements, and much more. I invite you to explore the content and engage with the materials as we embark on this exciting journey into the world of geoscience. Thank you for visiting!
